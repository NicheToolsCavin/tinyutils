FAQ

Will my Kindle read EPUB?

Newer Kindles support EPUB. Older models need MOBI format. You can email EPUB files to your Kindle address and Amazon converts them.

What about page numbers?

EPUB doesn't have fixed pages — text reflows. Chapter markers replace page numbers for navigation.

Can I convert a textbook PDF?

Textbooks with diagrams and multi-column layouts may not convert well. EPUB works best for flowing prose.
