Why convert to LaTeX?

  • Academic standard — Required by many journals and conferences
  • Beautiful typography — Professional-quality math and text
  • Bibliography support — Works with BibTeX
  • Version control friendly — Plain text source files

How to convert

  1. Go to TinyUtils Document Converter

FAQ

Will math equations convert?

Standard LaTeX math in Markdown (like $x^2$) passes through directly. The converter preserves math delimiters.

What about citations?

Add your \cite{} commands after conversion. The converter handles document structure; bibliography integration is a LaTeX-side task.

Can I use a custom document class?

The converter produces standard LaTeX. Change the document class in your .tex file after conversion.
