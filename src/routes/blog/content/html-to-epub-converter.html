Want to read web content on your e-reader? Convert HTML pages to EPUB for comfortable offline reading.

TL;DR

  • Upload HTML to TinyUtils Document Converter
  • Select EPUB as output
  • Download and load into your e-reader
  • Read articles offline with adjustable fonts

Why convert to EPUB?

  • Offline reading — No internet required
  • E-ink friendly — Perfect for Kindle, Kobo
  • Customizable — Change fonts, size, spacing
  • No ads or clutter — Just the content

How to convert

  1. Open TinyUtils Document Converter
  2. Upload your HTML file or saved webpage
  3. Choose EPUB format
  4. Click Convert
  5. Transfer to your e-reader

For best results

  • Clean HTML — Semantic markup converts better
  • Proper headings — Creates table of contents
  • Embedded images — External images may not load
  • Single article — Works better than full sites

Common use cases

Long-form articles

Save that 10,000-word article for reading on your morning commute. No WiFi needed.

Convert web-based documentation for offline reference when you're coding without internet.

Research reading

Collect web articles into ebooks for focused study away from browser distractions.

What converts well

HTML Element EPUB Result
<article> Main content
<h1> - <h6> Chapter/section headings
<img> (embedded) Inline images
<p> Paragraphs
<ul>, <ol> Lists

FAQ

Will styling be preserved?

Basic styling (bold, italic) transfers. Complex CSS layouts are simplified for reflowable text.

What about navigation menus?

Site navigation typically doesn't convert meaningfully. Extract the main content before converting.

Can I batch convert multiple pages?

Convert pages individually. For a multi-chapter ebook, consider merging the HTML first.
