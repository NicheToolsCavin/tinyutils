Why convert to ODT?

  • No license fees — LibreOffice is free
  • Open standard — ISO-certified format
  • Cross-platform — Works on Windows, Mac, Linux
  • Future-proof — Open standards last longer

How to convert

  1. Open TinyUtils Document Converter
  2. Upload your .docx file
  3. Choose ODT from the format list
  4. Click Convert
  5. Download and open in LibreOffice Writer

FAQ

Will my fonts look the same?

Font references are preserved. If the exact font isn't installed, LibreOffice substitutes a similar one.

What about tracked changes?

Track changes convert, but may display differently. Accept or reject changes before converting for cleanest results.

Can I batch convert multiple files?

Upload multiple files at once. The converter handles batch processing.
