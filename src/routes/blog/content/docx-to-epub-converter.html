Turning your Word document into an ebook? Convert DOCX to EPUB for Kindle, Kobo, and other e-readers.

TL;DR

  • Upload DOCX to TinyUtils Document Converter
  • Select EPUB as output
  • Download and transfer to your e-reader
  • Text reflows for optimal reading

Why EPUB?

  • Reflowable — Text adapts to any screen size
  • Reader-friendly — Adjustable fonts and themes
  • E-reader native — Standard ebook format
  • Navigation — Chapter-based table of contents

How to convert

  1. Go to TinyUtils Document Converter
  2. Upload your Word document
  3. Choose EPUB from the format list
  4. Click Convert
  5. Download and load into your e-reader

Prepare your DOCX for best results

  • Use heading styles — H1 for chapters, H2 for sections
  • Simple formatting — Avoid complex layouts
  • Embedded images — Ensure images are in the document
  • Single column — Multi-column layouts don't convert well

Document structure mapping

Word Element EPUB Result
Heading 1 Chapter
Heading 2-6 Subheadings
Bold/Italic Preserved
Images Embedded in EPUB
Page breaks Chapter breaks (when with H1)

FAQ

Will my images transfer?

Yes, embedded images are included in the EPUB. Make sure images are actually in the document, not just linked.

What about footnotes?

Footnotes convert to endnotes in EPUB format. That's standard for ebooks.

Can I sell this EPUB?

Yes, the EPUB output is production-ready. Add metadata in your publishing workflow as needed.
