Why RTF?

  • Universal — Opens on any computer, any OS
  • Editable — Full word processor editing
  • No special software — Built-in apps can open RTF
  • Lightweight — Smaller than DOCX for simple docs

FAQ

RTF vs DOCX — which is better?

DOCX preserves more formatting but requires Word or compatible software. RTF opens anywhere, even in basic text editors. Choose based on your needs.

Can I convert scanned PDFs?

Scanned PDFs need OCR first. Without OCR, there's no text to extract — just images of pages.

Will images transfer?

Basic image handling is supported. Complex image layouts may need adjustment.
