How to convert

  1. Go to TinyUtils Document Converter
  2. Upload your HTML file

FAQ

Will CSS styles transfer?

Basic formatting (bold, italic, colors) may transfer. Complex CSS layouts are simplified.

What about forms?

HTML forms don't convert to ODT form fields. Content text is extracted; interactivity is lost.

Can I convert back to HTML?

Yes, ODT can be exported to HTML from LibreOffice or through the converter.
