FAQ

Will styles be preserved?

Basic formatting converts. Complex styling may need CSS adjustments for your specific site.

What about images?

Images are referenced in the HTML. Extract them separately or host them at accessible URLs.

Is the HTML clean?

The converter produces clean, semantic HTML. No bloated markup or unnecessary spans.
