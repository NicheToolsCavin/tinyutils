Why convert to ODT?

  • Open standard — ODT is an ISO standard, not proprietary
  • LibreOffice native — Perfect compatibility with free office suites
  • Editable — Full word processor features after conversion
  • Portable — Works across platforms and applications

FAQ

Can I convert back from ODT to Markdown?

Yes, but complex formatting may not survive the round trip. ODT supports features Markdown doesn't have.

Will images in my Markdown convert?

Image references are preserved. Ensure images are accessible at their URLs.

Is ODT the same as ODF?

ODT is part of the ODF (Open Document Format) family. ODT specifically means text documents; ODF includes spreadsheets (ODS) and presentations (ODP).
