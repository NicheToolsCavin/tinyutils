FAQ

Will my scanned PDF convert?

Scanned PDFs are images, not text. You'll need OCR (optical character recognition) to extract text first. Google Docs offers free OCR.

Can I edit protected PDFs?

Password-protected PDFs must be unlocked first. Read-only PDFs may convert; edit-locked ones won't.

Why ODT instead of DOCX?

ODT is the native format for LibreOffice — no conversion needed when opening. If you use Microsoft Word, convert to DOCX instead.
