Contact TinyUtils

TinyUtils is a small, independent project maintained alongside other work. There’s no support team or ticketing system — just a person who cares about keeping these tools useful and reliable.

Best ways to reach out

The easiest way to report a bug or suggest an improvement is to open an issue on the project’s GitHub repository, or to send a short email with a clear subject line. When you write, include the tool you were using, what you expected to happen, and what actually happened.

What to expect

Because TinyUtils is maintained in spare time, replies may be delayed — especially around busy weeks. Most messages will get a quick acknowledgement first, then a follow-up once there’s time to investigate or ship a fix.

What this channel is for

Contact is welcome for bug reports, feature ideas, accessibility issues, and confusing UX. It’s not a good fit for detailed SEO audits, legal advice, or urgent production support. Tools are offered “as is”, and you’re responsible for how you use them.
