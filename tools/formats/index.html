Supported Formats

Our Document Converter can handle over 100 formats. Below are the most commonly used ones. If you need a format not listed here, contact us — we likely support it or can add it quickly.

Inputs

  • Markdown: .md, .markdown
  • Plain Text: .txt
  • HTML: .html, .htm
  • LaTeX: .tex
  • Word: .docx
  • OpenDocument: .odt
  • Rich Text: .rtf
  • PDF: .pdf (best with text-based PDFs)
  • EPUB/ZIP bundles: .epub, .zip

Outputs

  • Markdown: .md (multiple dialects available)
  • Plain Text: .txt
  • HTML: .html
  • Word: .docx
  • OpenDocument: .odt
  • PDF: .pdf
  • Rich Text: .rtf

Tip: For large or unusual documents, try one format at a time first, then add more via the advanced toggle in the converter.
