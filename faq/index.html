General

Are TinyUtils tools free to use?

Yes, all TinyUtils tools are free to use with no registration required. We're supported by optional privacy-respecting ads.

Do you store my files or data?

No. Files uploaded to our converter are automatically deleted after conversion. URL checks and sitemap comparisons don't store your data. All processing is temporary and secure.

What browsers are supported?

TinyUtils works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and mobile browsers. No installation or plugins required.

Dead Link Finder

How does the broken link checker work?

Enter your webpage URL and our tool crawls all links on that page, checking each one for HTTP status codes (200, 404, etc.), redirects, and errors.

Sitemap Delta

How do I use Sitemap Delta for migrations?

Compare your old and new sitemaps to see which URLs changed. The tool automatically suggests 301 redirects and generates nginx/Apache config files.

Wayback Fixer

How do I recover deleted webpages?

Paste your dead URLs into Wayback Fixer and it will search the Internet Archive for archived snapshots. You can then download or link to those archived versions.
