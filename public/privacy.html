Privacy

No login, no cookies by us. Minimal logs for debugging. Ads (AdSense) may appear on some pages if you configure AdSense; they are third‑party and have their own policies. URLs are processed to check status. Minimal logs may include submitted URLs and IPs for debugging and abuse prevention; logs are routinely purged.

We use Plausible (privacy-friendly analytics). You can toggle analytics off on the homepage; your choice is stored locally.

In the EU/UK, if you decline tracking, we may still show non‑personalized ads (no cookies/IDs) to keep the site free. You can change your choice anytime via your browser storage.
