What is TinyUtils?

TinyUtils is a small collection of focused, browser-based tools for people who work with websites every day — SEOs, developers, content editors, and site owners. The goal is to make common, fiddly tasks (like checking dead links or comparing sitemaps) fast, reliable, and easy to repeat.

How it’s built

The site is a static HTML/CSS/JavaScript front-end deployed on Vercel, plus a handful of serverless APIs for more complex work (like fetching metadata or converting documents). There are no user accounts or dashboards to manage — just tools you can open, use, and close again.

Privacy & safety

TinyUtils does not run user logins or sell personal data. Some tools need to fetch URLs you paste to do their job; those requests are made on your behalf and may briefly appear in server logs for abuse prevention and debugging. For the full picture, see the Cookie & privacy settings and Privacy pages.

Who it’s for

These tools are designed for people who are comfortable working in the browser and reading raw results. They’re intentionally minimal: no animations, no popups, and no onboarding flows. If you know what you’re looking for, TinyUtils aims to get you there quickly and stay out of your way.

How to help

If a tool saves you time, the best way to support TinyUtils is to share it with a colleague.
